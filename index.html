About My Internship

Company & Role

During the summer of 2025, I had the incredible opportunity to participate in SE Factory's Full-Stack Software Engineering (FSE) Bootcamp. This intensive 8-week program provided me with hands-on exposure to backend development, modern web technologies, and professional collaboration in a fast-paced learning environment.

Duration & Location

The bootcamp spanned 8 weeks from June 23 to August 14, 2025, with hybrid delivery including online sessions and in-person events at Beirut Digital District (BDD). I also participated in a hackathon that extended until August 18, 2025. This approach gave me valuable experience in both collaborative and independent work environments, while participating in intensive bootcamp-style learning.

Key Objectives

  • Master backend development with PHP, Laravel, and Node.js
  • Build a minimum of three advanced projects hosted on GitHub
  • Develop professional communication and teamwork skills
  • Learn AWS services for project deployment
  • Participate in live presentations and industry week events

Weekly Schedule & Commitment

This was an intensive bootcamp requiring full-day availability Monday through Friday. Each day had a different schedule with surprise attendance checks, requiring us to be prepared and available from 8 AM until 9-10 PM on weekdays. The program demanded complete dedication and flexibility to adapt to varying daily schedules.

Special Events - Industry Week (August 11-13, 2025)

During the final week, we participated in intensive tech talks and industry presentations at Beirut Digital District, providing valuable insights into real-world software engineering practices and career opportunities.
